## Quick Reference: Key Data Points

- TikTok 3-second hold: Videos maintaining 70-85% retention in first 3s get ~2.2x more views
- YouTube 30-second rule: ~70%+ retention at 30s correlates with strong algorithmic performance
- YouTube average view duration: ~50%+ significantly more likely to be recommended
- Chapters can increase total watch time by 10-30%
- 82-85% of people trust UGC and peer recommendations more than brand-created media
- UGC-style ads are 22% more effective on TikTok than polished brand creative

## Hook Types Reference
1. Pattern interrupt — breaks expectations visually or verbally
2. Curiosity gap — outcome without explanation
3. Contrarian claim — conflicts with conventional wisdom
4. Direct value promise — states specific benefit
5. Story entry — drops into high-emotion moment

## Scripting Level Decision Tree

- Legal/compliance-sensitive → Word-for-word
- Product announcements → Word-for-word  
- Educational (expert creator) → Hybrid
- Thought leadership (exec) → Hybrid or word-for-word
- UGC / testimonial → Beat sheet
- Podcast / interview → Outline with scripted open/close

## Retention Device Frequency
- Short-form: pattern break every 1-3 seconds, 1-2 open loops
- Long-form: structural break every 60-180 seconds, "coming up" teases at mid-video
- Podcast: segment transitions with verbal cues + optional music stings

## CTA Placement Rules
- Short-form: after payoff, final 3-5 seconds
- Long-form: after value milestones, hard CTAs at end-screens
- Podcast: mid-roll after natural content beats, host-read for authenticity

## Common Retention Curve Problems → Script Fixes
- Steep early drop-off → Tighten hook, remove preamble, pull payoff earlier
- Mid-video cliff → Restructure/compress slow section, add open loop before it
- Gradual decline → Add resets, "coming up" teases, vary pacing
- Spikes at chapters → Move popular topics earlier in future scripts
